## Standards for a Secure and Resilient Cloud Environment

Standards are the rules, best practices, and guidelines created by industry organizations, global entities, and governmental bodies that dictate how we design and operate our environments to create a foundational level of security for cloud services. They play a critical role in the protection of data, provide safeguards for privacy, ensure regulatory compliance, and provide guidance and frameworks for risk management. They can be vast in scope, tackling everything from data protection to access control, identity verification, incident response, and even encryption protocols.

Implementing security measures in cloud computing environments, organizations can better protect sensitive information from cyber threats or unauthorized access attempts.
